Operation R.A.I.N.B.O.W

Our aim to unite any and all fashion lovers from all subcultures seems big.

That's why we need your help!Whether you listen to different music or dress differently than others, we have created a community just for you!There will be events hosted, occasional concerts as well as a large varitey of games to play with! We'll be waiting for you and don't forget, you are welcome anytime!

You might be wondering, though: What does Operation R.A.I.N.B.O.W actually mean? Well, it stands for everything we stand for: Recruting, Accepting, Incorporating, Normalising, Becoming, Owning and reWorking alternative fashion through our business!

The employees at our arcade each have their own specialty when it comes to arcade games. As of now, there's about 100+ games, meaning there's something for everyone! From rhythm games to crane games, even console games too, you're sure to find something you like!

Join us today and help us create a safe environment for all alternative people! As times progressed, more alt people started gathering form all around the world and with a vision in mind, we give them a space where they can play and hang out with their friends as much as the want! From arcade machines to style swaps, this cute space was designed to foster a loving community!

You can also save your progress on the games you play by asking a staff member for an Aime, Banapassport or Konami card! That way you don't lose your best scores!
